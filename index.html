Why Project Pratyaksh?

Students often memorize definitions of Gravity and Light, but they rarely feel the wonder of it. Science becomes a burden of textbooks rather than a lens to view the universe.

We believe that you don't need a million-dollar lab to learn Physics. You just need a curious mind and the eyes to see.

  • Pratyaksha: Direct Perception over Theory.
  • Zero Cost: Accessible to every child.
  • Pure Science: Fostering deep questioning.

Class Modules

Tailored content for different developmental stages.

Module A

CLASSES 1 - 5
üåü

Class A1: Earth & Space Basics

Basics of Day/Night, Rotation, Revolution, and an introduction to 'What is Space'.

Class A2: Constellations & Planets

Connecting stars to make shapes (Constellations) and understanding our Solar System family.

Class A3: Stars & Beyond

What are stars? Why do they shine? A peek into the deep universe beyond our sun.

Module B

CLASSES 6 - 9
üß†

Class B1: Mechanics of Space

Gravity, Motion, Rotation vs Revolution, and understanding how orbits actually work.

Class B2: Stellar Astrophysics

Types of Stars, Constellations as navigation tools, and the scale of the Solar System.

Class B3: Cosmic Extremes

The fabric of Spacetime, Black Holes, Pulsars, and Quasars. Pushing physics to the limit.
