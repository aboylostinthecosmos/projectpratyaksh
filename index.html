Why Project Pratyaksh?

Students often memorize definitions of Gravity and Light, but they rarely feel the wonder of it. Science becomes a burden of textbooks rather than a lens to view the universe.

We believe that you don't need a million-dollar lab to learn Physics. You just need a curious mind and the eyes to see.

  • Pratyaksha: Direct Perception over Theory.
  • Zero Cost: Accessible to every child.
  • Pure Science: Fostering deep questioning.
